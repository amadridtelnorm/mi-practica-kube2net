Unified platform

One experience, every channel.

Conference, intercom, hotline, traditional telephony and messaging live in the same console — with the presence and priority your desk already understands.

01

Conference

Create private lines with an external dial-out number and unlimited participants. Add dial tones on the fly, without dropping the conversation.

02

Hoot & Holler

Open channels for simultaneous floor announcements. Push-to-talk, push-to-mute or latch — with voice quality measured in real time.

03

Intercom

Internal calls without external lines, in push-to-talk or latch mode. Any authenticated user with an assigned extension.

04

Hotline & private lines

Instant single-button connection. Color-coded priority, plus visual and audible alerts configurable per user.

05

Traditional telephony

Inbound and outbound SIP calls, native PBX or SBC integration via the K2N Gateway. Transfer, forwarding, hunt groups.

06

No geographic borders

Unlimited simultaneous communications in real time, federating multiple sites into a single centralized, resilient system.

AI-powered recording

Every conversation, captured, transcribed and audited.

Kube2net captures interactions across all channels in real time, transcribes them, and proactively detects compliance risks using artificial intelligence.

  • 01

    Automatic transcription and summary

    Turns every call or conference into searchable text. The AI generates an executive summary and distributes it only to authorized users.

  • 02

    Real-time risk detection

    Identifies anomalies, sensitive language and patterns that compromise regulatory adherence, before they escalate.

  • 03

    Redundant storage

    Cloud or on-prem schemes with dual sites. Complies with financial regulations across most jurisdictions.

  • 04

    Search by metadata or content

    Filter by name, conference, duration, date, status or user — or search exact words and phrases inside any recording.

Security & architecture

Cloud-native, orchestrated on Kubernetes.

High availability, automatic scaling and zero-downtime updates — with security built in from the core, not bolted on as a layer.

End-to-end encryption

WebRTC SRTP for all calls; TLS 1.3 on the control plane; AES-256 on stored recordings.

Resilient Kubernetes

Container orchestration with auto-scaling, zero-downtime deployments and per-namespace tenant isolation.

Hardened K2N Gateway

CentOS Stream 9 hardened by K2N, dual LAN interface, firewall with dynamic zones, OpenVPN site-to-site over SSL/TLS.

24×7×365 monitoring

Continuous metrics, intelligent thresholds, root-cause analysis, trend prediction and automated notifications.
